What Is Personal Color? (Understood in 5 Minutes)

The Core Concept: There Are Colors That Were Made for You

Your personal color is the palette of colors that harmonize most naturally with your skin tone, eye color, and hair color.

Think about it this way — the same shade of red:

  • On some people, a bright scarlet makes their face come alive
  • On others, a deep burgundy looks stunningly beautiful

That difference? That's what personal color is all about.

The 4-Season Framework: Which Season Are You?

Personal color is divided into 4 "seasons":

Spring (봄) — Warm and Bright

  • Color feel: Warm, clear, and light
  • Example shades: Coral pink, peach, light beige, olive green
  • Vibe: Radiant and fresh
  • Recommended lip shades: Coral tones, salmon pink

Summer (여름) — Cool and Soft

  • Color feel: Cool but gentle and muted
  • Example shades: Rose pink, lavender, soft grey, mint
  • Vibe: Calm and elegant
  • Recommended lip shades: Rose, berry, matte pink

Autumn (가을) — Warm and Deep

  • Color feel: Warm, rich, and earthy
  • Example shades: Terracotta, mustard, olive, brown
  • Vibe: Sophisticated and grounded
  • Recommended lip shades: Brown, orange-brown, burgundy

Winter (겨울) — Cool and Bold

  • Color feel: Cool, deep, and striking
  • Example shades: Pure red, black, purple, deep blue
  • Vibe: Intense and polished
  • Recommended lip shades: Pink-red, deep wine, pure red

Why Does This Matter?

1. Shopping becomes so much easier

What Happens During the Consultation

Step 1. Cleanse (5 min)

When you arrive, you'll do a light face wash at the cleansing station.

  • Remove all makeup
  • Skip skincare after (no essence or eye cream — your skin should be matte)
  • Tip: They'll take lots of photos during the draping process, so come with your hair and overall look polished (just no face makeup)

Step 2. Natural Light Setup (2 min)

The consultant seats you near a natural light source.

  • Why: To see how colors interact with your face in real-world lighting
  • Tip: The version of yourself by a window is the most accurate one

Step 3. Draping (40–50 min) — The Main Event

The consultant holds various colored drapes (fabric swatches) beneath your face and observes how each one affects your appearance.

The 3 things they're checking:

  • Skin tone shift: Does the color make your skin look clearer or muddy?
  • Vitality: Does your face look alive and energized, or tired?
  • Overall harmony: Does the color work with your eyes, hair, and skin together?

From 4 seasons to 16 sub-seasons:

  • Basic: Narrow down to Spring/Summer/Autumn/Winter
  • Detailed: Each season breaks into 4 sub-types:
  • Spring: Light, Clear, Warm, Bright
  • Summer: Muted, Light, Soft, Cool
  • Autumn: Muted, Deep, Warm, Saffron
  • Winter: Deep, Clear, Bright, Cool

Can you take photos?

  • Most studios allow photos during the draping process
  • Just ask first: "사진 촬영 괜찮으신가요?" (Is it okay to take photos?)
  • SNS tip: Draping shots are the most shareable — they look like a professional photoshoot

Step 4. Receive Your Result Card (2–3 min)

The consultant gives you a card or pamphlet summarizing your personal color.

What's on the card:

  • Your season (e.g., "Spring Light" or "Winter Bright")
  • Recommended color palette (lip, blush, eyeshadow tones)
  • Colors to avoid
  • Clothing color suggestions
  • Makeup tips

Practical Tips: Getting the Most Out of It

1. Before Your Consultation

  • Remove all makeup: Start clean from the base
  • Skip skincare: Avoid shiny products like eye cream or essence
  • Outfit: Wear neutral black or grey (lets the consultant see your skin tone clearly)
  • Timing: 2–5pm has the best natural light

2. Storing Your Result Card

  • Photo on your phone: Keep the card photo handy for shopping
  • Physical copy: Keep it card-sized in your wallet
  • App option: Some apps let you input your personal color results for real-time product lookup

3. Shopping Smart

  • Test on your face, not your wrist: Wrist skin tone is different from face skin tone
  • Check in natural light: Compare how it looks inside the store vs. outside
  • Try multiple shades within your season: Even within one season, compare 3–4 different tones

4. SNS Sharing Tips

  • Draping photos: The most "authentic proof" shot — Korean women love these
  • Result card + product: Share as "lipstick I chose for my personal color"
  • Before & after: Makeup comparison before and after the consultation = instant engagement
