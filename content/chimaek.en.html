Hangang Chimaek (치맥) Picnic Guide (3-2)

The Iconic Seoul Experience

> This is a must-do bucket list item for anyone visiting Seoul.

> Chicken + beer by the Han River = experiencing one of Seoul's most iconic moments firsthand.

Step 0. Checklist — Prep is Half the Battle

Chimaek (치맥) at Hangang (한강) looks simple, but trust me — prep is everything. Run through this before you head out.

Must-Have Items

Item Where to get it Price Notes
Picnic mat (돗자리) GS25, CU, any convenience store ₩3,000~5,000 Waterproof, foldable — highly recommended
Wet wipes (물티슈) Convenience store ₩2,000~3,000 Essential for greasy fingers — seriously, don't skip this
Trash bags (비닐봉지) Convenience store ₩1,000 Black (general waste) + yellow (food waste) for recycling
Portable charger (보조배터리) GS25, pharmacy ₩15,000~25,000 Switch your phone to battery-saving mode
Bug spray / sunscreen (모기약/선크림) Convenience store ₩5,000 Essential by season (summer / early autumn)

Nice-to-Have

  • Large plastic bag (for food waste)
  • Hand sanitizer
  • Small towel or hand towel
  • Phone stand (for photos)

Step 1. Setting Up the Delivery App (For Non-Korean Numbers)

Baemin / Baedal Minjok (배달의 민족) — Recommended ⭐⭐⭐

Why it's the best choice:

  • Works with foreign phone numbers (US, Europe, Japan, etc.)
  • Accepts international credit cards (VISA, Mastercard)
  • Most active delivery coverage in the Hangang delivery zones
  • You can call the rider to confirm your location

How to set it up (mobile)

1. Download the Baemin app (App Store / Google Play)

2. Choose "Continue as guest" when signing up (skips the Korean ID verification)

3. Phone verification → enter your international number (e.g., +1-555-XXXX)

4. Set a password (your choice)

5. Register a credit card

  • Go to Payment → Add credit card
  • Enter your international VISA / Mastercard
  • Input CVV and expiry date

6. Save delivery address → use the Hangang Park name + specific zone details (see below)

Yogiyo (요기요) — Backup App

Note: International numbers often can't complete the Korean carrier verification. Stick with Baemin as your main app.

Coupang Eats (쿠팡이츠) — Alternative

Pros:

  • English app interface available
  • Simple credit card payment

Cons:

  • Delivery zone coverage at Hangang is limited
  • Slightly pricier

Step 2. Setting Your Delivery Address at Hangang Park

✨ The Most Important Tip ✨

If your delivery address isn't set precisely, the rider will wander around the Hangang Park entrance trying to find you.

Save the exact address + landmark for whichever park you're visiting.

Yeouido Hangang Park (여의도한강공원) — Most Popular ⭐⭐⭐

3 Delivery Zones:

1. In front of Parking Lot 2 (제2주차장 앞) — Largest + closest to convenience store

2. Yeouido entrance ramp (여의도 나들목 앞)

3. Next to Mulbit Plaza entrance (물빛광장 진입구 옆)

Baemin address example (paste Korean as-is into the app):

```

여의도한강공원 제2주차장 앞 배달존

서울시 영등포구 여의도동 / 사직로 165

Yeouido Hangang Park, Delivery zone in front of Parking Lot #2

Address in EN: Sajik-ro 165, Yeouido-dong, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul

```

(Translation: "Yeouido Hangang Park, Parking Lot 2 Delivery Zone / Yeouido-dong, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul / 165 Sajik-ro")

What to tell the rider: "여의도한강공원 제2주차장 앞 배달존 내가 기다리고 있음"

(Translation: "I'm waiting at the Yeouido Hangang Park Parking Lot 2 delivery zone.")

Why it's the top pick:

  • Best views of the Han River
  • GS25 convenience store right next to the delivery zone (easy to grab extra beer and snacks)
  • Clear entry points — riders can find you easily
  • Perfect angle to face the sunset while you drink

Banpo Hangang Park (반포한강공원) — Moonlight Rainbow Fountain View ⭐⭐

Delivery zone:

  • Banpo area (check the exact zone map on the [Seoul Hangang Authority website](https://hangang.seoul.go.kr))

Baemin address example (paste Korean as-is into the app):

```

반포한강공원 (달빛무지개분수 근처)

서울시 서초구 신반포로11길 40

Banpo Hangang Park, near the Moonlight Rainbow Fountain

Address in EN: Sinbanpo-ro 11-gil 40, Seocho-gu, Seoul

```

(Translation: "Banpo Hangang Park / near the Moonlight Rainbow Fountain / 40 Sinbanpo-ro 11-gil, Seocho-gu, Seoul")

What to tell the rider: "반포한강공원 달빛무지개분수 무대 앞 잔디밭에 있음. 기다리는 사람 봤으면 전화줘"

(Translation: "I'm on the grass in front of the Moonlight Rainbow Fountain stage. Call me when you see someone waiting.")

Why you gotta try this:

  • Fountain show starts at 7:30 PM (free! Show times vary by season)
  • Seoul night skyline + fountain = the best photo spot, hands down
  • The moment that leaves foreign tourists absolutely speechless

Ttukseom Hangang Park (뚝섬한강공원) — Local Vibes ⭐

2 Delivery Zones:

1. Ttukseom entrance ramp (뚝섬 나들목 앞)

2. In front of Ttukseom Information Center (뚝섬 안내센터 앞)

Baemin address example (paste Korean as-is into the app):

```

뚝섬한강공원 배달존

서울시 광진구 뚝섬로 지역

Ttukseom Hangang Park, delivery zone

Address in EN: Along Ttukseom-ro, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul

```

(Translation: "Ttukseom Hangang Park Delivery Zone / Ttukseom-ro area, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul")

What to tell the rider: "뚝섬 안내센터 앞 배달존에서 기다리고 있음"

(Translation: "I'm waiting at the delivery zone in front of the Ttukseom Information Center.")

Why it's worth it:

  • Connected to Seoul Forest (숲속공원, Seoul Sup) — daytime forest walk + evening Chimaek combo
  • Close to Seongsu-dong (성수동) cafés and hotspots (you can explore first, then come here)
  • Genuinely local crowd — the "real Seoul" atmosphere

Mangwon Hangang Park (망원한강공원) — Quiet & Chill ⭐

Delivery zone:

  • Mangwon area (check zone coverage: [Hangang Authority map](https://hangang.seoul.go.kr/www/facility/map.tab))

Why it's a good pick:

  • Less crowded even on weekends
  • Mangwon-dong (망원동) has great cafés and restaurants nearby
  • Perfect if you want a relaxed, low-key picnic vibe

Step 3. Ordering Chicken

How to Order on Baemin

1. Search → type "치킨" (chicken)

2. Confirm your delivery zone address is set correctly

3. Pick a restaurant (see recommended brands below)

4. Choose your menu (spicy vs. original)

5. Decide quantity (2~3 people = 1 whole chicken, 4+ people = 2 chickens)

6. Special instructions → write "한강 배달존에 놔주세요" (please leave at the Hangang delivery zone)

7. Pay by credit card → done!

Order Tips

  • Timing: Usually 25~40 minutes from order to arrival (Hangang delivery zones have dedicated riders)
  • Check the weather: Rain can delay delivery (bring an umbrella just in case)
  • Avoid peak hours: Friday 5~7 PM = busiest time. Try to plan around it

Step 4. Beer + Snacks

Buy from a Convenience Store (Combine delivery + in-person shopping)

Pick up beer + snacks from a nearby convenience store and have everything ready before your chicken arrives.

Beer Recommendations

Korean Mainstream

  • Cass (카스): Most reliable, cheapest (₩4,500/can)
  • Terra (테라): Smooth taste, slightly pricier than Cass (₩4,800/can)
  • Kloud (클라우드): Light and refreshing (₩4,700/can)

Imported Beer (always stocked at convenience stores)

  • Corona (코로나, Mexico): Crisp lager, a favorite among international visitors (₩6,500)
  • Guinness (기네스, Ireland): Dark stout, popular around Halloween (₩7,000)
  • Heineken (하이네켄, Netherlands): European style, clean finish (₩6,000)

Convenience Store Snack Combos (by budget)

A. Budget Set (~₩5,000~7,000)

  • A couple of cheese sticks
  • One bag of salted chips (소금 프릭스)
  • Dried squid (마른 오징어) or dried ray (쥐포)
  • Ice cream

B. Mid-Range Set (~₩10,000)

  • Gyeranmari (계란말이, rolled omelette) + Jumeokbap (주먹밥, rice ball)
  • Choco Pie or Pepero (빼빼로)
  • Sausage / cheese balls
  • Vita 500 drink

C. Full-Spread Set (~₩15,000)

  • Gimbap (김밥, rice roll) — one roll
  • Gyeranmari (계란말이, rolled omelette) — one pack
  • Meatballs or frank sausage
  • ~~Ramyeon (라면)~~ → Cooking is prohibited at Hangang Park — see below!
  • Snacks + drinks

🚫 Important Rules

Cooking is not allowed at Hangang Park. (No boiling ramen, no grilling, etc.)

→ Only pre-cooked food from convenience stores is permitted.

Disposable container restriction: Starting in 2025, Seoul City is progressively restricting delivery food in single-use containers at all Hangang parks. Reusable container delivery or purchasing at on-site kiosks is recommended. Check the latest policy before your visit.

Step 5. Claiming Your Spot

Before You Arrive

1. Track "Rider's current location" in real time on Baemin

2. Call the rider when they're near the delivery zone (number is provided in the app)

3. Lay out your picnic mat on the grass (5~10 meters from the delivery zone)

Finding the Best Spot (Yeouido reference)

The ideal spot:

  • Directly facing the Han River
  • Facing the direction of the sunset
  • Enough personal space (not squeezed between other groups)

Spots to avoid:

  • Right next to the delivery zone (riders constantly coming and going = noisy)
  • Under a shade canopy (kills the ambiance)
  • Under tree branches (birds fly through)

Etiquette

Things you gotta do:

1. Sort your recycling (black bag for regular waste, yellow for food scraps)

2. Don't leave food out (attracts crows and rodents)

3. Keep music low (Bluetooth speakers are fine — just don't blast it)

4. Wind down after 11 PM (nighttime noise regulations apply)

Step 6. Enjoying Your Chimaek (치맥)

The Chimaek Ritual

1. Pour the beer: Wait for the foam to settle before drinking

2. Tear into the chicken: Keep those wet wipes within reach

3. One bite of chicken + one sip of beer = the essence of Korean Chimaek

Photo Tips for Instagram / SNS

For the best shot:

  • Shoot during golden hour (summer evenings 7~8 PM, spring/autumn 6~7 PM)
  • Get the Han River and a bridge in the background
  • Capture beer can + chicken + grass together
  • Take one wide shot from a distance (to capture the whole mood)

GOLDEN HOUR TIMING:

  • Summer (June~August): 6:30 PM ~ 8:00 PM
  • Spring / Early Autumn (April, May, September): 5:30 PM ~ 7:00 PM
  • Winter: avoid (gets dark way too early)

FAQ

Q: I have a foreign number — will Baemin really work?

A: Yes, it does. International credit card payments are fully supported. Just skip the Korean carrier verification and continue as a guest.

Q: Can I still get delivery late at night?

A: Hangang delivery zones operate until 11 PM. After that, riders don't come out — so plan accordingly.

Q: Will delivery still happen if it rains?

A: Technically yes, but expect 20~30 minutes of extra delay. Bring an umbrella and plan for it.

Q: Is drinking at Hangang actually allowed?

A: Yes — Hangang Park is one of the very few public spaces in Korea where outdoor drinking is legally permitted. No issues at all.

Q: Can I reserve a picnic spot?

A: Nope — it's first come, first served. On weekends after 5 PM, spots fill up fast. Get there early.

Q: Can I boil ramen at Hangang?

A: No. Cooking is prohibited. Stick to pre-cooked food from convenience stores.
